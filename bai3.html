Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods,Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a midium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan center every 20 seconds
